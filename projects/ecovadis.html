EcoVadis Top 5%
Rating — Supply

Led the end-to-end EcoVadis sustainability assessment process, achieving a Platinum-level recognition placing the organization in the Top 5% globally among 130,000+ assessed companies.

Background

EcoVadis is the world's largest and most trusted provider of business sustainability ratings, covering 130,000+ companies across 200+ industries and 175+ countries. A Platinum medal (Top 5%) signals leading performance across all four EcoVadis themes and is a key differentiator in global supply chain qualification.

This project spanned two assessment cycles, with the goal of improving from an initial Silver rating to Platinum — a jump that required systematic gap closure, evidence quality improvement, and cross-functional stakeholder alignment.

Process

The assessment covers four themes: Environment, Labor & Human Rights, Ethics, and Sustainable Procurement. Each theme is evaluated on documented policies, implemented actions, and measurable results.

I led a structured gap analysis benchmarking current documentation against EcoVadis criteria for each theme, then built a corrective action roadmap with owners, deadlines, and evidence requirements. Key workstreams included:

  • GHG inventory validation and Scope 3 methodology documentation
  • Supplier code of conduct rollout and compliance tracking system
  • Ethics hotline policy update and usage reporting
  • ISO 14001 gap assessment and management system documentation
  • Energy and water consumption dashboards with intensity metrics

Evidence Management

A significant portion of the effort was evidence management: identifying, organizing, and quality-checking the 80+ documents submitted across four themes. I built a tracking spreadsheet mapping each EcoVadis criterion to available evidence, gap status, and responsible owner — enabling real-time visibility for the sustainability team and senior management.

Result

The organization received a Platinum EcoVadis medal — Top 5% globally — in the 2023 assessment cycle. The rating is used in customer RFPs, investor ESG questionnaires, and CDP submissions. It has directly contributed to winning supply chain qualification with three major automotive and logistics customers requiring Platinum-level suppliers.
